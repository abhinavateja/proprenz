About Us

Welcome to Proprenz Bio-Tech

Proprenz Bio-Tech Pvt Ltd is based at Bachupally situated in Hyderabad, Telangana State, India. Proprenz mainly concentrated on the animal feed additives and we use state-of-the-art technology in producing probiotics and enzymes.
The people behind the company are expertise over 25 years in the field and our main motto is to design the quality products at affordable prices for both local and international markets. We adopted a policy to produce pure, safe high effective probiotics and enzymes for Poultry, Piggery, Cattle, sheep and Aqua Farms. Every effort, scientific and technical will be made to comply with the requirements of the market.
